Intro

Welcome to the Landing Page for the Hampden Sydney Peninsula Club! Here we have set out to make communication across our social media posts, coordination with the leadership team, and ultimately more engagement with our beloved school more accessible!

Through the incredible guidance of The College, and particularly the Alumni Office, we hope to have events atleast once a quarter. These events strive to engage the Hampden-Sydney Community at large in the Peninsula area. We hope to see you at our next event!

Leadership Team

The H-SC Peninsula Alumni Club is led by volunteer alumni who work together to coordinate events in the area. Our team consists of individuals who are passionate about Hampden-Sydney College and are dedicated to fostering a strong alumni community in the Peninsula area. We are always looking for new members to join our team and help us plan events and engage with alumni in the area. If you are interested in joining the leadership team, please contact us at Our Club Email(hscpeninsula@gmail.com)

About

A sophomore at Hampden-Sydney College, Gyabaah is pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics, with a minor in Spanish. Originally from Ghana, West Africa, he brings a global perspective to campus as an international student.

Gyabaah is actively involved in the Hampden-Sydney community as a mentor in the "Belonging On The Hill" (BOTH) mentorship program, where he supports new students in their transition to college life. Passionate about community engagement, he takes pride in his role within the alumni club, working to ensure current students on the Hill have a meaningful and memorable college experience.
